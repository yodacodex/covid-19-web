WHAT IS COVID-19 WEB?

This is a website to help track covid through live data and provide support for students. We believe that it is important to keep track of data and present it to the people in order to raise awareness of the current circumstances in our daily lives. We will also provide any useful information to help your mental wellbeing during this period.
